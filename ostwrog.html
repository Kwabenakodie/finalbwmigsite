Ost-Wurttemberg

The migration of infants showed varying growth rates throughout the years, with significant fluctuations from a decrease of 1.8% to an increase of 76.5%. The migration of children exhibited a mix of growth and stability, with growth rates ranging from 3.6% to 49% during the observed period. The migration of teenagers experienced both positive and negative growth rates, with fluctuations ranging from -8.5% to 12.1%. The migration of adults remained relatively stable, with growth rates ranging from 1.5% to 8.2% over the years. The migration of older adults demonstrated consistent but modest growth rates, ranging from 1.3% to 7.5% during the observed period. In summary, the migration patterns in Ost Wurttemberg for different age groups between 2012-2013 and 2020-2021 varied. While infants, children, teenagers, and older adults experienced fluctuations in growth rates, the migration of adults remained relatively stable.
